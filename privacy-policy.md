# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 22, 2026

## Introduction

Calendário Menstrual ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.
This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.
By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## Information Storage

Calendário Menstrual is designed to store your personal records locally on your device.
Information such as menstrual cycle history, reminders, and other data entered into the App remains exclusively on your device.
The developer does not collect, access, store, or transmit this information to external servers.
No user account or registration is required to use the App.

---

## Personal Information

The App does not require you to provide personal information such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Home address
- Government identification
- Photos

Any health-related information entered into the App is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to the developer.

Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically, such as:

- App interaction information

This information is collected directly by Google and is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy.
You can learn more about how Google collects and processes data here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

https://support.google.com/admob

## Cookies and Similar Technologies

The App itself does not use cookies.
However, Google AdMob and other Google services may use cookies or similar technologies to provide, personalize, and measure advertisements.
For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

---

## Data Deletion

Since all personal data entered into the App is stored exclusively on your own device, there is no process for requesting deletion of data from the developer.
The developer cannot access, modify, recover, or delete any information stored within the App.
If you wish to permanently delete your data, you may:
- Clear the App's storage through your Android device settings; or
- Uninstall the App from your device.
These actions will permanently remove all locally stored data associated with the App.

---

## Data Security

Because your information is stored locally on your device, protecting your data also depends on the security of your device.
We recommend using your device's security features, such as a PIN, password, or biometric authentication.
Although reasonable measures are taken to provide a secure application, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.
The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
If you believe that a child has provided personal information through third-party advertising services, please contact the respective service provider directly.
